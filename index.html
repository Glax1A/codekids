Easy-to-use IDE

With our user-friendly IDE software, tackling coding practice problems becomes a breeze. With features designed for learners of all levels, our IDE empowers students to write, debug, and test code seamlessly, fostering a smooth and enjoyable learning experience.

Instant Feedback & Detailed Results

After running your code in our IDE, you'll receive instant feedback with a progress bar indicating the number of test cases passed. Each test case is accompanied by an expected result, allowing you to compare your output efficiently. Additionally, our detailed results display your output alongside the expected result, making it easy to identify any differences and iterate on your solution effectively.

What's the story behind our initiative?

Founder & President - Aarush Kute

Our initiative is deeply rooted in my personal journey, ignited by a pivotal moment in 4th grade when I first encountered an Arduino Uno. The sheer thrill of creating something tangible with technology sparked a profound interest that would shape my future. As I delved deeper into Arduino projects, I found myself not only fascinated but driven to push the boundaries of what I could achieve.


With each project came new challenges, and I discovered the power of programming to bring my ideas to life. This dedication and practice led me to remarkable successes, earning numerous awards on a national level and even placing in the top 40 globally in prestigious coding contests.


These experiences instilled in me a desire to share the wonders of technology with younger generations. I realized the transformative impact that early exposure to STEM fields can have, and I'm committed to providing opportunities for kids to develop a love for technology just as I did. Our initiative aims to inspire and empower the next generation of innovators, fostering a community where curiosity thrives and dreams take flight in the boundless world of technology.
